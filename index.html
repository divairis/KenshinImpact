About

IMMERSE YOURSELF in Kenshin Impact, a role playing game that features an open wold environment and action based battles! Play as the traveler from another world and set out in a journey to search for your long lost sibling. As you progress, meet curious characters and discover all the hidden secrets of Zalea.

PICK THE CHARACTERS that you want to fight by your side! Assemble your team of 5 with diverse characters - each with one of a kind capabilities and powers. You may also invite your friends to defeat formidable enemies and solve challenging puzzles.
